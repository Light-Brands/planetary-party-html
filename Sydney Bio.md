*An interactive journey where your visions and ideas will become seeds for collaboration in the Planetary Party ecosystem*

Traditional philanthropy has reached its limits \- too often directing resources to those with access to institutional power (or to those with experience writing grants and reports) rather than to the grassroots leaders living the challenges philanthropy seeks to solve.

Flow Funding offers a systemic alternative, moving money directly through individuals and community-rooted groups who act with responsiveness and trust.

In this session, Syd will introduce [Kinship Earth](https://www.kinshipearth.org/)‚Äôs spin on Flow Funding, taking a bioregional approach, or in other words, entrusting grassroots leaders who are deeply embedded in the ecological, cultural, and social realities of their place to deploy resources in service of their communities and ecosystems. 

As co-founder of non-profit [Permatours](https://www.permatours.org/) \- a permaculture-action network that hosts learn-by-doing festivals throughout Turtle Island \- Syd will share how gatherings can be catalytic for creating the more beautiful world our hearts know is possible.

After setting the aforementioned context, Syd will segway to the interactive portion of the session, centering her latest purpose / passion project where all of her worlds are converging: the Planetary Party.

* practicing deep listening and trust building

* culminating in a festival where community members convene in assembly, celebrate through art and cultural exchange, and decide together how to heal their place and deploy resources.
