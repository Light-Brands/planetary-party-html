a new myth of humanity is emerging

The old story—of extraction, separation, and endless growth—is dissolving. A new narrative is being born: humanity as a regenerative species, celebrating our interdependence with all life, creating abundance through coherence.

from extraction to regeneration

Everywhere, the mycelial networks of regeneration are spreading beneath the surface— communities growing food, restoring land, building new economies, experimenting with governance, creating art that heals.

Yet these efforts remain fragmented, isolated, underresourced. What's missing isn't effort—it's coherence. It's the living architecture that allows us to sense together, see together, and flow resources toward what works.

what is the planetary party?

the party is three things at once

A Meme

An open-source cultural invitation for anyone, anywhere to gather and celebrate regenerative action. The meme spreads virally—anyone can throw a Planetary Party.

"Let's throw a party for the planet."

A Living System

A federated ecosystem of bioregional networks, guilds, dashboards, and Flow Funds— designed to support communities in implementing the full Protocol.

Key Components:

  • Bioregional Intelligence Dashboards
  • Local Flow Funds
  • Nine functional Guilds
  • Bioregional Embassies

A Cultural Technology

A replicable process (the Protocol) that communities can use to transform festivals into tools for collective intelligence, decision-making, and regenerative infrastructure.

The Five-Phase Protocol:

  • SENSE — The Roots
  • SEE — The Mycelial Map
  • FLOW — The Water Table
  • CELEBRATE — The Bloom
  • REGENERATE — The Spiral

core principles

Living systems principles that guide how the Planetary Party operates

Bioregional Rootedness

Every Party is rooted in a specific place—a watershed, a cultural landscape, a bioregion. Change happens at the scale of place.

Federated Sovereignty

Each bioregion is autonomous, making its own decisions. The global network provides support, not control.

Distributed Intelligence

Intelligence emerges from the network—communities sharing learnings, patterns, and resources across bioregions.

Celebration as Catalyst

Joy, art, and collective celebration aren't add-ons—they're core technologies for transformation and community building.

Positive-Trace Infrastructure

Every gathering leaves behind lasting regenerative infrastructure—gardens, governance models, Flow Funds, strengthened relationships.

how we operate

a federated living system connecting bioregional movements

the living architecture

The Planetary Party operates as a decentralized mycelial network, with each bioregion functioning as a sovereign node connected through shared protocols and mutual support.

  • Bioregional Dashboards: Real-time intelligence systems
  • Flow Funds: Trust-based capital flowing to grassroots leaders
  • Guilds & Partners: The coordination field connecting efforts
  • The Party: Festivals, assemblies, and action labs bringing it all to life
