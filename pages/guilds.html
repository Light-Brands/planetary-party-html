The Guilds

Our Nervous System

The Planetary Party is powered by Guilds: autonomous, interconnected teams stewarding different dimensions of the ecosystem. Each Guild is an organ, carrying a unique function within a larger living body.

The Guilds Form the Nervous System of the Planetary Party

Together, they inform the nervous system—sensing, signaling, regenerating, and weaving coherence across bioregions. Each Guild is a mycelial node, carrying a unique function within a larger living body.

What Guilds Are

Guilds are autonomous working groups that steward specific capacities and deliverables. They operate with sovereignty while maintaining coherence with the whole ecosystem.

Each Guild develops frameworks, tools, and protocols that bioregions can access and adapt to their unique context.

Why Guilds Work

  • Distribute Intelligence: Expertise lives at the edges
  • Enable Autonomy: Teams self-organize and innovate
  • Maintain Coherence: Shared protocols ensure alignment
  • Scale Organically: Guilds grow based on capacity
  • Cross-Pollinate: Knowledge flows between domains

How to Join

Guilds welcome individuals and organizations who bring relevant expertise and are committed to regenerative values.

Each Guild has its own onboarding process, rhythms, and contribution pathways. Explore the Guilds below and find where your gifts align.

Bioregional Intelligence Guild

▼

The Brain — Sensing place through stories, data, land, and culture

The Seers

This Guild develops the Bioregional Intelligence Dashboards and the sensing capacities behind them. They weave ecological, cultural, economic, and social indicators into shared maps that translate complexity into clarity.

Core Functions

  • Develop bioregional indicator frameworks (ecological, social, economic, cultural)
  • Design and build intelligence dashboards and visualization tools
  • Support bioregions in gathering local data, stories, and patterns
  • Guide the sensing → mapping → coherence arc
  • Translate complexity into actionable insights
  • Track impact and progress over time

Key Deliverables

  • Bioregional Intelligence Dashboard templates
  • Community sensing methodologies and toolkits
  • Data collection protocols and best practices
  • Indicator libraries for different contexts
  • Training programs for local data stewards
  • Integration with existing mapping and GIS tools

Who Should Join

Data scientists, ecologists, ethnographers, storytellers, geographers, systems thinkers, community researchers, and anyone passionate about making the invisible visible.

They help communities see themselves—coherently.

The Anchor Circle: Upholding the Integrity of the Whole

The Muscles — Moving the body and upholding the integrity and coherence of the whole.

What the Anchor Circle Does

The Anchor Circle is a coordination body representing each Guild's voice. It maintains ethical, cultural, and ecological integrity across the ecosystem.

The Anchor Circle Represents:

  • Each Guild's voice and needs
  • Shared protocols and alignment
  • Coherence between culture, governance, and action
  • Evolutions of the Planetary Party Protocol's global framework

They help the whole organism stay attuned to itself.

The Core Fractal Ensures Coherence Across the System

The Guilds are represented by the Core Fractal, a coordination body that maintains ethical, cultural, and ecological integrity. It supports cross-pollination between Guilds and stewards shared resources and intellectual property.

Cross-Pollination

Guilds regularly share learnings, resources, and insights. What one Guild develops can inform and enhance the work of others.

Examples:

  • Storytelling amplifies Bioregional Intelligence insights
  • Technology supports Flow Fund transparency
  • Governance frameworks inform Event Production protocols
  • Living Systems solutions feed into Dashboard indicators

Shared Resources

All Guild outputs are stewarded as commons—accessible to bioregions through onboarding to ensure coherence and ethical alignment.

Shared resources include:

  • Frameworks and methodologies
  • Templates and toolkits
  • Training programs and curricula
  • Case studies and documentation
  • Technology platforms and code

Maintaining Integrity

The Core Fractal (Anchor Circle) ensures that as the Planetary Party scales, it maintains coherence with core values:

  • Ecological regeneration
  • Social equity and justice
  • Cultural sovereignty
  • Participatory governance
  • Data sovereignty
  • Celebration and joy
